What we do ?

Project

To combat with bullies, we are developing an application which focus on the surrounding of the victim, empowers them to speak up the bully incident. The mission of Asankari is to support the schools to solve the bullying problem by identifying the victims of bullying and to learn people more about bullying.

  • Application: StopBully
  • Target group: People who surrounding the victims
  • Status: On processing
  • Applying area: Oulu, Finland
  • Website: www.asankari.fi

DESCRIPTION

An anti-bullying mobile application that empowers the bystanders and victims to speak up. It is a new way to transform the way people report in this digital age. We have 2 applications, one for students use to send anonymous report, the other, for the schools to receive and analyze report data.

  • Technical info: Angular4, Typescript, Ionic, Wordpress and Adobe Illustrator
  • Material info: Coffee, energy drink, Dumle, Chocolate Fazer and love
  • Testing place: Mailkula and Teuvo Pakula
  • Testing clients: 20 students and teacher
